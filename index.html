Who Am I?

My name is Poyraz. I'm currently in Year 12, living in the UK, with my sights set on studying Computer Science at university and building a career as a game developer.

What drives me isn't just a love of games — it's the feeling they create. That rare moment where you stop being a player and start feeling like you're truly living inside another world. That feeling is what I want to build for other people.

I have a deep passion for creating new ideas and crafting experiences that genuinely resonate. For me, game development isn't about the technology alone — it's about making something that moves people, something they can step into and feel.
